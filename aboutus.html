About Us

Our Story

Founded in 1995 in Nairobi, Kenya, Travel Creations has grown from a small travel agency into a trusted name in both corporate and leisure travel. With a passion for exploring the world and a commitment to exceptional service, we’ve built a reputation for delivering personalized travel solutions tailored to our clients’ needs. Over the past three decades, we’ve served thousands of travelers, blending local expertise with global reach.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform travel into seamless, memorable experiences. We strive to provide innovative solutions, competitive pricing, and unparalleled support, ensuring every journey—whether for business or pleasure—exceeds expectations. Our dedication to sustainability and community impact drives us to create a positive footprint wherever we go.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of certified travel experts brings unmatched professionalism and care. With continuous training and decades of collective experience, we craft journeys tailored to every traveler’s needs.

Our Partnership

Travel Creations is the exclusive representative of Reed & Mackay in Kenya. Together, we provide world-class corporate travel management, global account solutions, and 24/7/365 traveler support.

Our Core Values

Our foundation is built on Respect, Integrity, Excellence, Communication, and Commitment — values that guide every journey we plan.
