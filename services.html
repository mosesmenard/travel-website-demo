Our Services

Corporate Travel

Travel Technology

Partnered with Travelport, we deliver cutting-edge solutions like Agentivity for real-time traveler tracking. Our Smartpoint platform consolidates airline fares, hotel rates, and car rentals—saving your business both time and money. Access global accommodations via Travelport Rooms and More and ground transportation through Cabforce.

Travel Management

Our tailored solutions drive down costs, increase efficiency, and ensure seamless experiences. With our Management Information System (MIS), you’ll access detailed reporting—from cost savings analysis to passenger summaries.

  • Flexible payment options
  • Continuous staff training
  • Dedicated Key Account Managers
  • Corporate agreement support

Leisure Travel

Safaris

Experience unforgettable African safaris—from Kenya’s Masai Mara to Tanzania’s Serengeti. Our planners design immersive wildlife adventures with top-tier lodges and guides.

Vacation Packages

Explore curated vacation packages for beach escapes, cultural tours, and adventure trips worldwide. We handle flights, stays, and activities—offering competitive rates and personalized service.
