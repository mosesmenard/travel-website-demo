Why Travel Creations is the agency for you!

Back to Blog

For organisations with frequent travel requirements, the value of a trusted travel management partner cannot be overstated. The question then becomes: why partner with Travel Creations?

We are not merely a travel agency — we are a strategic business partner. Our mandate is to support your company’s objectives by delivering seamless, efficient and cost-effective travel solutions.

With a proven record of excellence, we align our expertise with your business needs to deliver measurable value. Below are 10 compelling reasons why Travel Creations is the partner of choice for forward-looking organisations.

  1. 1

    Data analytics and consulting services

    Beyond bookings, we give you actionable insights through analytics, consultancy, and risk management — helping you control costs and protect your travelers.

  2. 2

    Tailor-made solutions

    No one-size-fits-all. We study your travel patterns and contracts to design policies and partnerships that deliver maximum value for your business.

  3. 3

    Savings

    Our global affiliations and strategic partners give us unmatched negotiating power, securing competitive fares and driving measurable cost efficiencies.

  4. 4

    Experience

    With over 30 years in the industry, we combine deep expertise with proactive fare monitoring and an ability to adapt quickly to changing markets and technologies.

  5. 5

    Technology

    From Agentivity for real-time traveler tracking to our online booking engine, we use smart tools that streamline processes and enhance duty-of-care compliance.

  6. 6

    Supporting Services

    We offer more than flight reservations — we handle hotels, insurance, car rentals, visas, excursions, and full event management, making us your single travel solutions hub.

  7. 7

    24/7 Support

    Emergencies don’t wait, and neither do we. Our team is available 24/7/365 to resolve last-minute changes and provide personal assistance worldwide.

  8. 8

    The A-Team

    Our people are our strength. Carefully selected and highly trained, our staff deliver professionalism, expertise, and a genuine commitment to your success.

  9. 9

    Global Partnerships

    As Reed & Mackay’s exclusive partner in Kenya, and a proud member of IATA, KATA, KATO, and KTF, we bring global reach with local expertise.

  10. 10

    Giving Back

    We care about community and clients alike. Through CSR initiatives and our loyalty programs, we reward your travel and impact society positively.

We could give so many more reasons but we will stop there for now. We are always ready and willing to answer any questions you may have, feel free to contact us. Keep on the lookout for more articles about travel and Travel Creations.
