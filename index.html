Data Security Standard

We accept all major credit cards for flight bookings and related services. To ensure your transactions are safe, we comply with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) — a global framework that safeguards sensitive cardholder data.

PCI DSS compliance means our booking systems, payment gateways, and processes are built to prevent data breaches, reduce fraud, and give you confidence that your personal and financial information is handled securely at every step.
